Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Overview

Aria's Sanrio Adventure is a free casual game designed for players of all ages. We take privacy seriously, especially for younger players.

Data We Collect

Player Name: Stored locally on your device only. Used for the in-game leaderboard. Never sent to third parties.

Game Progress: Your collection, scores, outfits, and stars are stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage).

Leaderboard Scores: If you play the game, your chosen display name and score may be submitted to our anonymous leaderboard. No personal information, email, or device identifiers are collected.

Custom Avatar Photos: If you upload a photo as your avatar, it is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to any server.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No email addresses
  • No real names (only chosen display names)
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies
  • No advertising data

Children's Privacy

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only data stored is gameplay progress on the local device.

Third-Party Services

This game does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our website at aria-cinnamoroll.vercel.app.
